About Perfect Strata Maintenance

Perfect Strata Maintenance only deals with Strata Managers, Building Managers and Developers! With over 10 years’ experience in the Strata Maintenance industry, ranging from residential homes to large commercial and industrial complexes, we possess the required level of expertise to carry out all types of Strata Maintenance work you may require. We are determined to provide unparalleled service and support to every one of our clients.

We Provide :
  • On the spot problem solving and enquiry handling
  • Creative, innovative, user-friendly processes and procedures
  • Various building management tasks at no extra charge
  • Assistance in defect rectification work between the developer and owner’s corporation.
  • Regular quality control inspections and detailed reports
  • Recommendation of services accompanied with relevant quotes
These solutions allow you to :
  • Increase productivity > By spending less time liaising with owners and contractors as we handle all requests on your behalf.
  • Increase customer satisfaction > By handling your owners for you.
  • Reduce operating costs > Through our cost effective packages

Mical Hader- Director

A multifaceted and results driven Operations & Sales professional, Mical built the company from the ground up. She has over 17 years of experience in construction finance and real estate property management working with BF Money (previously known as Byblos Finance), a multi award-winning commercial brokerage firm. Working with the finance company since its inception, she remained with the business for over 16 years managing various departments. As a licensed real estate agent, she also became a director and shareholder of “Byblos Realty” working as the licensee in charge.

This experience afforded her intrinsic industry knowledge and a respected presence in the field. She has a reputation for delivering exceptional sales and managing business processes and has a keen eye for detail. With a flair for building rapport and a foundation of trust with clients, she is a team-spirited leader with an ability to connect with people on high levels.
