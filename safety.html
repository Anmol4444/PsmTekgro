SAFETY & QUALITY

Safety and Quality

  • Perfect Strata Maintenance is committed to ensuring and upholding your safety. We conduct a Site Safety Inspection prior to the commencement of any service where any and all safety hazards and risks are highlighted, analysed and then corrected.
  • ur team is trained to recognise any safety hazards and report them promptly with photos.
  • Every team member complies with the Safe Work Method Statement (SWMS) and understands the correct procedure and hazard controls that need to be in place.
QUALITY CONTROL

Our commitment to providing unparalleled service to all our clients means we ensure the highest level of quality in everything we do. We continually strive to better ourselves and improve through:

  • Evaluating and recording customer satisfaction
  • Continuous Staff training and development
  • Conducting internal audits and site inspections
